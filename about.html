Orphange Campaign 2024: Milon Mela(I added this here)

On March 2nd, volunteers from Hope Bangladesh visited Families For Children, a Canadian non-profit aiding children with disabilities. The day featured joyful activities like singing, a cultural show, and an art competition where the children's talents were celebrated with prizes. Essential school supplies were gifted to support their education. Sharing a meal helped build strong connections, making the day memorable and highlighting the power of compassion and outreach.

Winter Campaign 2023: Share the Warmth(I added this here)

Hope Bangladesh successfully completed Phase 2 of Share the Warmth, reaching over 500 underprivileged families in Kaultia, Gazipur. They distributed over 3,500 donated clothes to men, women, and children, providing much-needed warmth. This generosity has inspired them to consider extending Phase 2.
